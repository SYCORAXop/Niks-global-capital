Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 2026 | NIKSGLOBAL CAPITAL LLC

1. Introduction

Welcome to NIKSGLOBAL CAPITAL LLC (operating as "The Indian Property Show"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website, register for our VIP global exhibitions, or engage with our real estate advisory services.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect personal data that you voluntarily provide to us when expressing an interest in obtaining information about our services or properties. The personal information we collect depends on the context of your interactions with us and the choices you make, and may include:

  • Identity Data: First name, last name, title.
  • Contact Data: Email address, telephone numbers (including WhatsApp), and country of residence.
  • Investment Preferences: Real estate interests, budget ranges, preferred locations (e.g., Mumbai, Pune, Delhi NCR), and property types.
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type and version, time zone setting, and operating system.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the personal information collected via our website for a variety of business purposes described below:

  • To facilitate VIP account creation and event registration for our Global Exhibitions.
  • To assign you a dedicated regional portfolio manager to assist with your investment inquiries.
  • To send administrative information to you, including updates regarding event schedules or property availability.
  • To communicate with you directly via WhatsApp, Phone, or Email regarding Tier-1 developer projects.
  • To deliver targeted advertising and real estate market reports relevant to your stated interests.

4. Sharing Your Information

As a global advisory connecting NRIs with Indian real estate, we may process or share your data based on the following legal basis:

Business Partners & Developers: We may share your information with our partnered Tier-1 developers (e.g., Lodha Group, Godrej Properties, Kalpataru, Rubrick Constructions) solely for the purpose of facilitating the real estate transactions and meetings you have explicitly requested.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for their independent marketing purposes.

5. International Data Transfers

Because NIKSGLOBAL CAPITAL LLC operates internationally (with HQ in Sheridan, Wyoming, USA, and offices globally), your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed by us and our affiliates in the United States, Europe, the Middle East, and India. We take all necessary measures to ensure your data is treated securely and in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

6. Security of Your Information

We have implemented appropriate technical and organizational security measures designed to protect the security of any personal information we process. However, despite our safeguards and efforts to secure your information, no electronic transmission over the Internet or information storage technology can be guaranteed to be 100% secure.
